ABOUT PERPUSNESAT

Experience the wonders of PERPUSNESAT, located within the educational realm of SMKN 1 Gunungsindur. Here's what you should know:

  • Operating hours: Monday to Friday, 08:00 AM to 04:00 PM.
  • Mandatory textbook borrowing for all SMKN 1 Gunungsindur students.
  • Non-mandatory book borrowing allows a 14-day loan period, extendable with staff notification. Late returns will incur a fine.
  • Kindly adhere to the library rules while inside.

Welcome to PERPUSNESAT. Together, let's foster a comfortable and joyful atmosphere. Happy reading!
